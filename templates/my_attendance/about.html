About Attendance Manager

Empowering students to take control of their academic journey through smart attendance tracking.

Who We Are

Attendance Manager is a comprehensive attendance tracking solution designed specifically for students. Our mission is to help students stay organized, maintain their attendance goals, and succeed academically.

We understand the challenges students face in keeping track of their classes and maintaining the required attendance percentage. That's why we built a simple yet powerful tool that sends timely reminders, tracks your progress, and provides valuable insights into your attendance patterns.

Whether you're managing attendance for multiple subjects or just want to stay on top of a single course, Attendance Manager adapts to your needs with customizable notifications, detailed analytics, and intuitive tracking features.
